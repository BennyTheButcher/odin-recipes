"Yummy Yogurt Breakfast Parfait"

Light and nourishing Yogurt Parfaits for someone on the go!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of strawberries
  • 1/2 cup of blackberries
  • 1/2 cup of blueberries
  • 4 cups of vanilla yogurt
  • 1 cup of granola
  • Small plastic or glass cups

Follow these steps:

  1. Dice strawberries in to smaller pieces.

  3. Repeat layers one more time and refrigerate until read to serve.
